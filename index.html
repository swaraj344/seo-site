Check out some frequently asked questions about SEO

What is SEO?

SEO or Search Engine Optimization is the terminology used for all those techniques that help you rank higher in search engine results. Lets take a simple example. Say you run a bakery in Noida. When someone wants types bakery in Noida in Google, your website should come up in the first few results. SEO refers to all the tools and techniques to get you there.

What is the role of SEO in marketing?

When you invest in SEO, your website starts showing up when people are searching for relevant businesses. Given that so many people today make purchase decisions using an online search, investing in SEO can result in a huge boost to your business. The best part is all traffic that comes to your website through SEO is organic traffic. Which means that unlike online advertising, you dont have to pay money every time someone clicks on your ad and comes to your website.

Why do I need SEO services?

3.5 billion searches are done on Google every single day. And 88% of people who search for a local business on their smartphone, call or visit the business within 24 hours. All of this simply means that in order to run a competitive business today, you simply need to have a strong online presence. And SEO is the only way to build a long-term online presence that is sustainable.

How do I pick the right keywords?

Heres how we go about picking the right keywords for our clients:

  • Understand the customer

As a first step, we try to understand our customers needs and pain points to figure out what theyre likely to type into the search bar. This should always be the first source of finding the right key words.

  • Competitor research

We then SEO tools to see which keywords competitors are ranking for. This helps broaden our research and include keywords we may have missed out on.

 

  • Find long-tailed keywords

Long-tailed keywords are a combination of different words and phrases, and tend to be longer and more detailed than regular keywords. These keywords attract lesser traffic, but the traffic will be more relevant and competition will typically be much lesser.

 

  • Analyse

We dont stop after developing an initial list of keywords. We work with these keywords while constantly monitoring results to see which keywords are doing well. We then remove and add keywords accordingly, keeping the SEO process dynamic.

Its important to keep in mind that SEO is a process that yields results in the medium and long-term. We dont believe in black hat SEO techniques that give great results for a few days but end up hurting your website in the long run. Our SEO strategy is built on sustainable techniques that we execute relentlessly over a long period of time. Having said that, most clients start seeing substantial results anywhere between 12 to 20 weeks.

Do you guarantee rankings?

Given the nature of SEO, there is no way to give a guarantee of rankings as a lot of that depends on the nature of your business and existing competition. Having said that, our expertise lies in understanding our clients business to come up with niches that are less competitive but attract high-quality traffic.

What is a SERP?

SERP or Search Engine Results Page refers to the web pages that are showed to users when they type a query into a search engine like Google or Bing. Every SERP is unique, even when the same search query is typed on the same search engine. This is because search engine algorithms customise the search results based on a users location, social settings, and browsing history. The whole purpose of SEO is to get your website to rank highly on the SERP.

What is a Google Penalty?

A Google Penalty is when your websites ranking gets negatively impacted due to an update to Googles search algorithm or a manual review of your website. The Google Penalty sometimes happens simply because the algorithm gets updated in a way that your website is not optimised for. Once you discover what the possible change is, you can then optimise your website accordingly and climb up in the rankings again. Sometimes, a Google Penalty is imposed because the website has used black-hat SEO techniques to quickly increase its rankings. This kind of penalty can be very hard to overcome. Its best to have a SEO strategy that uses in-depth and ethical techniques, so that it shows sustainable results in the long run.

Why do my Google rankings fluctuate?

Fluctuation in Google rankings is actually fairly common, especially when youre in the bottom half of the first page or on pages 2,3,4,5, etc. The rankings also fluctuate right after you first enter into the top 5 results. Its only after youre consistently in the top 5 results for 2-3 months, that the rankings tend to stabilise. So some amount of fluctuation is a natural part of the Google algorithm, since it customises search results based on the users location and history. The only time you need to really worry is if all your web pages lose rankings fairly drastically.
